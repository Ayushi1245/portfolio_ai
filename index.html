About Me

Building Minds, One Model at a Time

I'm Ayushi — an AI & ML enthusiast pursuing my MCA at Graphic Era Hill University, Dehradun. My passion? Language models, NLP pipelines, and anything that pushes the boundaries of what machines can understand.

I'm deeply fascinated by Large Language Models — how they're trained, how they reason, and how they can be fine-tuned for real-world tasks. From tokenisation and embeddings to attention mechanisms and prompt engineering, I love exploring every layer of the LLM stack.

I also enjoy building computer vision systems and classic ML pipelines. A Data Science Intern at Shriram Software Solutions gave me real-world grounding. Beyond code, I paint, sketch, play basketball — and always stay hungry for the next new technology.
